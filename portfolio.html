Nonprofit Build Grow Thrive

Comunidad Viva

Community Organization • Portland, OR

The Challenge

Comunidad Viva is a grassroots nonprofit connecting Latino families in Portland with health, education, and legal resources. Despite serving over 200 families a year, they had no website, no online presence, and relied entirely on word-of-mouth and paper flyers. Volunteers were managing event signups through text messages, and donors had no way to give online.

The Journey

Build

We launched a bilingual Squarespace website with program descriptions, an events calendar, a volunteer signup form, and a donation page linked to their PayPal. We set up their custom domain (comunidadviva.org), professional email through Google Workspace, and SSL certificate. The team received a 2-hour training session on updating content themselves.

Grow

Next, we built their digital foundation: Google Business Profile so they appear in local searches, basic SEO so families searching "recursos para latinos Portland" can find them, Google Analytics to track which programs get the most interest, and a Mailchimp newsletter to keep donors and volunteers engaged. We also set up their Instagram and Facebook pages with consistent branding.

Thrive

As the organization grew, we migrated them to a custom WordPress site with advanced features: an interactive program finder, impact reporting pages with infographics, integrated donation processing through GiveWP, and a full WCAG accessibility audit to ensure the site serves all community members. We completed their GuideStar and Charity Navigator profiles to build credibility with institutional funders.

The Results

  • 300% increase in online donations within 6 months
  • 150+ new volunteer signups through the website
  • 1,200 monthly website visitors from organic search
  • 45% newsletter open rate (nonprofit average is 25%)
  • 2 new grant opportunities sourced through GuideStar visibility
"Before Raíz Digital, we were invisible online. Now families find us through Google, donors give with one click, and we just received our first grant that specifically mentioned our digital presence. They understood our community because they're part of it." — María Gonzalez, Executive Director (fictional)

Services Delivered:

  • Bilingual template website with custom domain & email
  • Google Business Profile & local SEO
  • Analytics, social media & newsletter
  • Custom WordPress rebuild with GiveWP donations
  • WCAG accessibility audit
  • GuideStar / Charity Navigator listings

Artist Build Grow Thrive

Elena Torres Art

Visual Artist • Mixed Media & Murals • Portland, OR

The Challenge

Elena Torres is a mixed-media artist and muralist whose work explores identity, migration, and belonging. She was selling original pieces through Instagram DMs and local art fairs, but had no website, no way to sell prints online, and no email list. Collectors who discovered her murals around Portland had no way to find her other work or purchase it.

The Journey

Build

We created a clean Squarespace portfolio site showcasing her work across collections: murals, mixed media, and commissions. The site included a bio page telling her story, a gallery with high-resolution images, a contact form for commission inquiries, and links to her social profiles. We registered elenatorresart.com and set up professional email.

Grow

We built out her digital foundation: optimized her Instagram bio and content strategy with a consistent posting schedule, set up a ConvertKit newsletter for collectors and fans (with an automated welcome sequence), configured Google Analytics to track which collections get the most interest, and implemented basic SEO so people searching "Portland muralist" or "Latina artist Portland" find her work.

Thrive

When Elena was ready to sell prints, we migrated her to a custom WordPress site with WooCommerce and Printful integration. Now visitors can browse her gallery and order museum-quality prints, canvas wraps, and postcards — all fulfilled and shipped automatically through Printful. We also added a WCAG accessibility audit, an exhibition calendar, and a press page featuring her mural projects and media coverage.

The Results

  • $4,200 in print sales within the first 3 months
  • 800+ email subscribers from zero
  • 5x increase in commission inquiries
  • 2,000 monthly website visitors from organic search and social
  • 3 gallery exhibition invitations sourced through online visibility
"I went from selling art out of my trunk at markets to shipping prints across the country while I sleep. Raíz Digital didn't just build me a website — they built me a business. And they explained everything in a way that actually made sense." — Elena Torres, Artist (fictional)

Services Delivered:

  • Portfolio gallery with custom domain & email
  • Social media strategy & branding
  • SEO, analytics & newsletter with automation
  • Custom WordPress + WooCommerce rebuild
  • Printful print-on-demand integration
  • WCAG accessibility audit
