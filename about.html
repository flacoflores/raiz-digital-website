About

The story behind Raíz Digital

Why "Raíz"?

Raíz means "root" in Spanish. It reflects what we do: help organizations build strong digital foundations that support growth for years to come.

Just like roots anchor a tree and draw up nourishment, your digital presence should anchor your mission and help you reach the people who need you.

The Story

Raíz Digital was founded with a simple observation: nonprofits and artists do incredible work, but they're often underserved by the tech industry.

Big agencies charge big prices. DIY tools leave you frustrated. And the rapid pace of AI development has left many organizations wondering how to keep up — or if they even should.

We believe technology should amplify your mission, not complicate it. That means building things that work, teaching you how to use them, and being there when you need help.

We're not trying to be the biggest agency. We're trying to be the most helpful partner for organizations doing good in the world.

How We Work

Clarity Over Complexity

We explain things in plain language. No jargon, no upselling, no unnecessary features. Just what you need to succeed.

Education First

We'd rather teach you to fish than keep you dependent on us. When you understand your tools, you're empowered to grow.

Long-Term Thinking

We build for sustainability, not quick wins. Your website should serve you for years, not months.

Accessible Pricing

Mission-driven organizations often work with tight budgets. We offer nonprofit discounts and flexible payment options.

Human Connection

You'll work directly with us — no account managers, no hand-offs. We know your organization because we're in it together.

Thoughtful AI

AI is a tool, not magic. We help you use it thoughtfully, ethically, and in ways that genuinely serve your mission.

Based in Portland

Raíz Digital is based in Portland, Oregon — a city with a rich community of nonprofits, artists, and people who care about making the world better.

We work with clients locally and remotely. Whether you're across town or across the country, we're here to help.
